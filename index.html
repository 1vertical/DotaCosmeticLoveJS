Table Design Changes

Added

  • Added new columns.

Changed

  • Reordered columns to make comparisons easier.
  • Data source changed from gamepedia.com to fandom.com (former URL reroutes to the fandom.com URL).

Removed

  • "Broken down?" column is removed. All heroes are currently released. For the uninitiated, this column tracked which heroes were ready/partially ready/not ready for getting new equipment.

Notes

  • Golden number means this hero has a(n) item(s) with duplicates that has gold textures.
  • Add more items after the time of publishing for a realistic count of items - I count by hand and don't web crawl the source site and it is possible that items were added after I was done counting.
  • Sets doesn't necessarily include items made for all slots. Examples: Single items, Arcanas, early lifetime sets - typically a set with 3 items instead of an item for all slots, etc.
  • Each hero has their own amount of slots - this can inflate/deflate the ratio. This means it can affect the actual total number of items per hero.
  • There is no "Workshop Sets (Past)" column because I didn't record it back in the day.
  • Column explanations:
    • Hero - The hero in question.
    • Slots - The total amount of slots for the character. Note: this may have been changed and will be indicated in parenthesis.
    • Has Arcana - The hero has an arcana item or not.
    • Has Persona - The hero has a personal item or not.
    • Workshop Sets (Present) - The current total sets that is on the workshop. Note: This is an unreliable number as there are sets with items that are: not items (junk) and items that don't fit with the aesthetic of the game.
    • Published Sets (Past) - The last recorded total of published sets.
    • Published Sets (Present) - The current recorded total of published sets.
    • Published Sets (Difference) - The total difference between old and new published sets.
    • Total Love (Past) - The last recorded total of published items.
    • Total Love (Present) - The current recorded total of published items.
    • Total Love (Difference) - The total difference between old and new published items.
    • Notes - Notes about the particular hero's items, trends, general information regarding design, etc.
