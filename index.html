Greetings folks! I'm back. I've made a new "analysis" of Dota 2's wardrobe. What has changed since the last... *checks publish date of last analysis* ...6 years?! What. Anyway, without further wasting time. Here ya go! :)
Please note: This webpage contains a table and is best viewed on a desktop.

Table Design Changes

Added

  • Added new columns. (See Notes to see what they are and what they represent.)

Changed

  • Reordered columns to make comparisons easier.
  • Data source changed from gamepedia.com to fandom.com (former URL reroutes to the fandom.com URL).

Removed

  • "Broken down?" column is removed. All heroes are currently released. For the uninitiated, this column tracked which heroes were ready/partially ready/not ready for getting new equipment. Check the past publication for the list broken down status of all the heroes.

Notes

  • Column Explanations
    • Hero - The hero in question.
    • Slots - The total amount of slots for the character.
    • Arcanas - The hero has an arcanas or not.
    • Personas - The hero has a personas or not.
    • Taunts - The hero has taunt animations or not.
    • Kinetic Gems - The hero has kinetic gems or not.
    • Workshop Sets - The current total sets that is on the workshop. Note: This is an unreliable number as there are sets with items that are: not items (junk) and items that don't fit with the aesthetic of the game.
    • Published Sets - The past (COLOR), present (COLOR) and difference (COLOR) in recorded total of published sets.
    • Total Love - The past (COLOR), present (COLOR) and the difference (COLOR) in recorded total of published items.
    • Notes - Notes about the particular hero's items, trends, general information regarding design, etc.
  • So many damn items - Add more items after the time of publishing for a realistic count of items - I count by hand and don't web crawl the source site and it is possible that items were added after I was done counting.
  • Variable items per set - Sets doesn't necessarily include items made for all slots. Examples: Single items, Arcanas, early lifetime sets - typically a set with 3 items instead of an item for all slots, etc.
  • Variable slots - Each hero has their own amount of slots - this can inflate/deflate the ratio. This means it can affect the actual total number of items per hero.
  • No Past Workshop Sets Column - There is no "Workshop Sets (Past)" column because I didn't record it back in the day.
  • Taunts - Taunts are not part of the slots column.
  • Weapon Packs - Weapon packs are considered sets and counted in the Set columns.
  • Arcanas - Some arcanas are either a set or a single item.
  • Gold - Some heroes have their total items inflated with golden variants which exist as separate items.
